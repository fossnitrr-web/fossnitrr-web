Intro

FOSS NITRR is a collaboration to contribute to Open Source, led by students at NIT Raipur. We are striving to get a very good coding culture. Many students and people from and outside of NIT Raipur are part of this society. FOSS NITRR is open for anyone who is willing to contribute their part.

We have people working in different areas. Following are the areas we are working on right now.

  • Machine Learning and AI
  • Web Development
  • Android Development
  • Competitive Programming

We are working hard to create a framework and road map to learn all of these things within limited time but with maximum comprehension.

Get connected to us. Drop your issues at our Github.

Competitive Programming

We are announcing a Competitive Programming Challenge. 100 days of competitive programming!

You have to do competitive programming for 100 days. Keep it simple, one problem a day if you're just getting started. There is no barrier to join. If you think you can do better, just do it and daily share your updates with us.

You can use twitter, Facebook, WhatsApp, discord or whatever. Use #100DaysOfCompetiveProgrammingNITRR

The top 5 people (who make the most impact on others) of this challenge will get a prize money of total 10 thousand INR.

The challenge begins from 10th of August. And the first phase will end on 30th November. The prizes will be distributed on this date. But you can start any time any day in between. Making most impact matters.

Note: Prize are for NIT Raipur folks only. But anyone can be a part of this challenge.

Tip: You can create a github repo and share it regularly to make more impact. Also, tell more people about it. Help them if they get stuck.
